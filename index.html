About the Show

Appalachia's Brand.
Born in Kentucky.

The New Frontier Show is more than a podcast — it's a platform for the voices, stories, and spirit of Appalachia. From the hollers of eastern Kentucky to the ridgelines of the Blue Ridge, we bring you conversations with the people who live it, build it, and carry it forward.

Whether it's trail culture, small-town entrepreneurship, or the music that echoes through the mountains, every episode is a window into a way of life that's as alive today as it's ever been.
