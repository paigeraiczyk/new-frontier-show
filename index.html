About the Show

Appalachia's Podcast,
Born in Kentucky.

We're Joshua and Jared — two Kentucky natives who grew up in the hills, hollers, and small towns of Appalachia. We built New Frontier as a brand to represent where we come from, and The New Frontier Show is the next chapter of that story.

This podcast started because we wanted a space to have real conversations about the culture, people, and places that shaped us. From sitting on the tailgate talking about what it means to grow up in eastern Kentucky, to sitting down with the entrepreneurs, artists, and everyday people doing incredible things in our backyard — this show is our way of putting Appalachia on the map the way it deserves.

We're not just telling stories. We're building something — for the people who've always been here, and for the ones just now discovering what makes this place special.
